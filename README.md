## üìñ Feature Guide

### 1. Kanban Board
The core of **Stuff** is the board view.
- **Create Tasks**: Click the `+` button in any column to add a task.
- **Move Tasks**: Drag and drop tasks between columns to update their status.
- **Edit Columns**: Double-click any column title to rename it.
- **Manage Sections**: Collapse sections to focus or delete them if no longer needed.

### 2. Task Details & Subtasks
Click any task to open the detail view:
- **Rich Text**: Add bold, italics, lists, and headings to descriptions.
- **Subtasks**: Add subtasks, check them off, and reorder them with the arrow keys.
- **Labels**: Categorize tasks with custom color-coded labels.
- **Priorities**: Set Low, Medium, or High priority to change the task indicator color.

### 3. Calendar View
Visual timeline of your work:
- **Reschedule**: Drag and drop any task to a new day to instantly update its due date.
- **Status Indicators**: Tasks are color-coded (Red for High Priority/Overdue, Green for Done).
- **Day Preview**: Click on a day with many tasks to see a focused list.

### 4. Project Management
- **Workspaces**: Create separate workspaces for different contexts (e.g., "Work", "Personal").
- **Custom Icons**: Hover over a project in the sidebar and click the icon to choose a custom emoji.
- **Renaming**: Rename projects or workspaces to keep things organized.

### 5. Command Palette (`Cmd+K`)
Press `Cmd+K` (or `Ctrl+K` on Windows) to open the command palette. From here you can:
- **Navigate**: Switch between Board, Calendar, and Analytics views.
- **Search**: Find tasks instantly.
- **Actions**: Toggle dark mode, export data, or manage labels.
- **Switch Workspaces**: Jump between different project workspaces.

### 6. Analytics Dashboard
Gain insights into your work habits:
- **Scope Control**: Filter analytics by **Current Project**, **Current Workspace**, or **All Workspaces**.
- **Priority Breakdown**: See visual progress bars for High, Medium, and Low priority tasks.
- **Up Next**: A quick list of upcoming deadlines.

### 7. Custom Filters & Views
Create dynamic views to focus on specific work:
- **Create**: Click **New Filter** in the sidebar.
- **Filter By**:
    - **Priority**: Show only High, Medium, or Low priority tasks.
    - **Label**: Select one or multiple labels (e.g., "Design", "Backend") to see related tasks.
- **Scope Control**: Each filter has a **Globe Icon** to toggle between searching the current workspace or **All Workspaces**.

### 8. Data Management
Your data is yours.
- **Auto-Backup**: Enable auto-backup in Settings to save snapshots to your local file system (requires permission).
- **Export**: Generate a `.json` file of your entire workspace state.
- **Import**: Restore your data on a new device or browser.

### 9. Customization
Make **Stuff** your own:
- **Theme**: Switch between **Light** and **Dark** mode.
    - Go to **Settings** (bottom of sidebar) ‚Üí **Appearance**.
    - Or use `Cmd+K` and type "Toggle Theme".
- **Project Icons**:
    - Hover over any project in the sidebar.
    - Click the icon (e.g., üìÅ) to open the emoji picker and choose a new one.
